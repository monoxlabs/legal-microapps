# Privacy Policy — Christmas Countdown
**Last updated:** May 19, 2026
This privacy policy describes how information is handled in the mobile application **Christmas Countdown** (the "App"), developed by Carlos (the "Developer").
## 1. Information the App does NOT collect
The App **does not collect, store, or transmit** any personally identifiable data. Specifically:
- It does not require registration or a user account.
- It does not ask for your name, email, phone number, or location.
- It does not access your calendar, contacts, camera, microphone, or photos.
- It has no server of its own. All usage data (preferences, opened doors of the advent calendar) is stored **only on your device** through the App's local storage.
## 2. Information handled by third-party services
The App uses the following third-party services, which may independently collect information in accordance with their own privacy policies:
### Google AdMob (advertising)
The App displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect information such as the device's advertising identifier (AAID), device type, operating system, approximate location, and ad interactions, in order to show relevant ads.
Before displaying personalized ads, the App requests your consent through Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP), in accordance with the GDPR and EU privacy laws. You can change your choice at any time from the App's settings.
Google's privacy policy: [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
### Google Play Billing (in-app purchases)
Purchases within the App (such as "Remove ads") are processed exclusively through Google Play Billing. The Developer **never sees or stores** your payment data. Google handles the entire transaction.
Google Play policy: [https://play.google.com/about/play-terms](https://play.google.com/about/play-terms)
## 3. Local notifications
The App may show you notifications reminding you to open the advent calendar doors. These notifications are generated **locally on your device**. They are not sent from any server and do not involve data transmission.
You can disable notifications at any time from the App's settings or from your device's settings.
## 4. Minors
The App is not specifically directed at children under 13. It does not knowingly collect information from minors. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided information, please contact us.
## 5. Changes to this policy
This policy may be updated occasionally. The "Last updated" date will reflect any changes. Continued use of the App after changes implies acceptance of the updated policy.
